Discover Needham

Rich Heritage Since 1711

Needham's roots trace back to 1680 as part of Dedham, officially incorporating in 1711. Named after the town of Needham Market in Suffolk, England, the community developed around farming and evolved into a thriving Boston suburb. Today, Needham combines historic charm with modern amenities, creating an ideal environment for families.

Perfect Balance

Needham offers the perfect balance of suburban living with easy access to Boston. The town is renowned for its excellent schools, vibrant downtown, extensive recreation programs, and strong sense of community. With beautiful parks, top-notch facilities, and engaged residents, Needham provides an exceptional quality of life.
